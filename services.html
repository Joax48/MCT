    Transport

    Specialized in transport, which has complete equipment (gen set, semi-trailers) of its own and subcontracted, for the transport of the following:

    • Cargo of perishable products.

    Container Handling Equipment

    Our sister company Cotrosa has 5 container cranes, 2 for empty ones and 3 for loaded ones, it operates in Limón and Caldera.
    The cranes have a maximum lifting capacity of 45 tons in the case of loaded containers.
    Counting on highly renowned clients in the country such as Sociedad Portuaria de Caldera and RADA Caldera.

    Temporary parking rental

    We have an area set up for the storage of vehicles, machinery of all kinds, chassis and containers. They have officers and security cameras, it has easy access to the main road, close to the main ports of the country.
