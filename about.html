    Moonlight´s history

    Moonlight began in 1994, when José Rafael Garrón Velázquez and Juliette Madrigal Monge bought their first truck, where Mr. Garrón began working as an independent driver in the early years in the Sixaola area (Limón, Costa Rica).

    Our commitment to the environment

    There is a contract signed in 2012 with FONAFIFO (National Forest Financing Fund), based on Forestry Law No. 7575, in which they commit to providing environmental services for the development of forest protection activities in the 142.6 hectares that includes our Vizcaya farm, which is located in Costa Rica, Limón.
