    Our commitment to the environment

    Environmental care

    In accordance with Law 8839, the law for comprehensive waste management, Moon light sends its waste motor oils and similar to ECO TRADING, S.A., a company authorized by the Ministry of Health, as well as the proper disposal of the filters, thanks to the Expert Diesel company that provides us with this service of the different types used by our equipment, as provided in Art. 38, subparagraph c), where said waste is subjected to a recycling process and recovery of the value of its waste.

    Reforestation

    There is a contract signed in 2012 with FONAFIFO (National Forest Financing Fund), based on Forestry Law No. 7575, in which they commit to providing environmental services for the development of forest protection activities in the 142.6 hectares that includes our Vizcaya farm, which is located in Costa Rica, Limón, Siquirres, Dos Bocas de Parismina farmhouse, these services include the reforestation of the farm, which was almost entirely cut down by squatters who were trying to seize it ; At the end of the reforestation, care will be taken to avoid events such as the one previously occurred.

    The cultivation of abaca fiber as raw material is a certified and sustainable product that is used for products that reduce environmental impact since it has a growing market because it helps to reduce climate change.
